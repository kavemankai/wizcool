## Full description (4000 chars max)

**Your crew doesn't level up. Your gear breaks down.**

Fringe Ledger is a turn-based tactics game about working-class salvage
crews on the frontier — XCOM-style squad combat with a GBA-era look, built
around one idea: damage doesn't kill your people, it destroys their
equipment.

**GEAR IS THE CHARACTER**
Every hit your crew takes fractures armour, then weapons, then breaks them
for good. Fractured gear loses its edge mid-mission; broken gear costs real
credits at the Terminal Hub. Push deeper for salvage, or extract before the
repair bill eats the contract?

**NO DICE. NO MISSES. NO EXCUSES.**
Every attack lands — but cover, range, and flanking decide whether it's a
CLEAN HIT, a GRAZE, or a DEFLECTED scratch. See the exact result before you
commit. Out-think the board, not the random number generator.

**THE FIRST REPOSSESSION**
A frontier colony has defaulted on its corporate lease, and Meridian
Recovery Group holds the writ. Walk the site, tag the assets, and haul the
lease core home — while colony security, desperate looters, and the rival
Vanguard Salvage crew work the same claim.

- 5-mission contract campaign, with bonus contracts to unlock
- 3 crew specialists with weapon abilities: arc pulse, corrosive burst, brace
- Deterministic graze-tier combat with full shot preview
- A rival crew with names, faces, and a grudge
- Gear economy: field-patch in mission, fence and repair between jobs
- Premium and offline: no ads, no IAP, no data collection, no internet

One more contract. What's the worst that could happen to the gear?
